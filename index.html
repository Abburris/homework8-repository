Question: How many schools are in the danger zone if a Nuclear power plant were to explode?

If a nuclear power plant were to explode, anything within a 50 mile radius would be exposed to radiation.

Anything within a 10 mile radius of a power plant will be exposed to the radioactive plume. Anyone within a 50 mile radius may be exposed to radiactive particles from the plume.
